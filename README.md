# KNGPay

KNGPay provides crypto-funded payment cards that allow users to spend digital assets like USDT and BTC as card balance.

Cards can be added to Apple Pay and Google Pay and used worldwide wherever supported payment networks are accepted.

## What KNGPay Offers

- Crypto cards funded with USDT (TRC20) and BTC  
- Standard, Gold, and Black card tiers  
- Apple Pay & Google Pay support  
- Worldwide online and contactless payments  
- Direct crypto-to-card funding (no P2P marketplaces)

## How It Works

1. Users start the official KNGPay Telegram bot  
2. Choose a card tier and desired balance  
3. Send the exact crypto amount shown  
4. Card balance is loaded after on-chain confirmation
